Cannabinoids

  • THCv (Tetrahydrocannabivarin)
    • Minimal psychoactivity
    • Typically blended with a psychoactive base
  • Δ10 (Delta-10-Tetrahydrocannabinol)
    • 20-30% as potent as Δ9
    • Sativa-like effects
  • Δ8 (Delta-8-Tetrahydrocannabinol)
    • 40-55% as potent as Δ9
    • Used as a base for more rare cannabinoids
  • HHC (Hexahydrocannabinol)
    • 50-65% as potent as Δ9
    • Significanly more noticable effects compared to Δ8
    • Hydrogenated version of THC
  • HHC-O (Hexahydrocannabinol acetate)
    • 50-65% as potent as Δ9
    • Sativa-like effects
  • HHCa (Hexahydrocannabinol acid)
  • THCa (Tetrahydrocannabinolic acid)*
    • 90-100% as potent as Δ9
    • Converts to Δ9 when decarbed
    • Non-psychoactive until it's decarbed (ie. heat, vape)
    • Legality: gray area; be careful; hemp-derived only; look for "High THCa" hemp flower/concentrates
  • Δ9 (Delta-9-Tetrahydrocannabinol)**
    • Main psychoactive compound in cannabis
    • Legality: Federally legal in quantities less than .03% by dry weight (LoOpHoLe?!?!)
    • Legality (pt. 2): Legal in medicinal/recreational states
    • Virtually unlimited strain options and effects
    • JUST F*CKING LEGALIZE IT
